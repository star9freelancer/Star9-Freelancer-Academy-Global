## Key Rules

1. **First lesson is always unlocked**
   - Students can start immediately

2. **Sequential unlocking across entire course**
   - Not just within modules
   - Must complete Lesson 1 before Lesson 2
   - Must complete Module 1 before Module 2
   - Must complete Week 1 before Week 2

3. **Quiz lessons require 80% to pass**
   - 4 out of 5 questions correct
   - Automatic completion on pass
   - Must retake on fail

4. **No skipping ahead**
   - "Next Lesson" button disabled until current complete
   - Locked lessons show error on click

5. **Progress persists**
   - Saved to database
   - Syncs across devices
   - Falls back to localStorage if needed
